About ToDoApplication

ToDoApplication is a simple Django Application Developed By Poorna Chand(@me) in the year of 2019. This Application serves with the basic features that a Simple ToDo App need. Personally, I use most of the ToDo Apps throughout my day to organize things.

At the time of developing this Django App(ToDoApplication), I have been learning Django web framework for a week. I strongly believe in the statement "The best way to learn something is by doing." So to learn the working of Django I just want to mimic the ToDo App Feature as it could help me in understanding the working environment of Django.

The time taken for me to build this application was about 14 Hours(worked around 6 Hours a day)-quite long duration, Right? I have been working since 4 Years in Back-End-Development. As I am not good at Front-End-Development, it took me a long duration to come up with a simple UI that I have presented to you as ToDoApplication. Hope you all like it.
